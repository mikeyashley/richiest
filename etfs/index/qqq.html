QQQ Overview - The Invesco QQQ Trust

The Invesco QQQ Trust (QQQ) is a passively managed ETF that aims to track the performance of the NASDAQ-100 Index, providing investors with exposure to some of the largest non-financial companies listed on the NASDAQ stock exchange. The ETF holds a diversified portfolio consisting primarily of equity securities of companies included in the index.

QQQ's investment strategy focuses on replicating the performance of the NASDAQ-100 Index, which includes leading technology and growth companies. The fund's portfolio offers investors a convenient way to gain exposure to the technology sector and other innovative industries represented in the index.

As of May 15, 2024, QQQ has delivered impressive long-term performance with an average annual return exceeding that of many traditional index funds. Investors can monitor QQQ's current performance and price movements on financial platforms like Yahoo Finance.

While past performance is not indicative of future results, QQQ's focus on high-growth companies within the NASDAQ-100 Index may appeal to investors seeking exposure to the innovation-driven segments of the stock market.

QQQ - Portfolio and Asset Allocation

The Invesco QQQ Trust (QQQ) is an ETF that provides exposure to a diversified portfolio of stocks, primarily focusing on the technology sector but also encompassing other industries. Its top holdings include industry leaders such as Microsoft Corp., Nvidia Corporation, Apple Inc., Amazon.Com, Inc., Meta Platforms, Inc., Broadcom Inc., Alphabet Inc. Class A, Alphabet Inc. While technology dominates the portfolio, the inclusion of other sectors ensures diversification and balance in the fund's holdings.

Investors looking for exposure to leading technology companies alongside a mix of other industries may find QQQ to be a suitable option, offering the potential for long-term growth and diversification across sectors.

Additional Information and Risks

Invesco QQQ Trust, an exchange-traded fund (ETF) tracking the NASDAQ-100 Index®, offers exposure to large-cap growth stocks primarily listed on the NASDAQ stock market. Managed by Invesco Capital Management LLC, QQQ is rebalanced quarterly and reconstituted annually to align with the index's composition and weighting. This actively traded ETF provides investors with comprehensive trading data and the opportunity to invest in a broad-based large-cap portfolio.

Morningstar, a renowned investment adviser, assigns QQQ an overall rating of 5 stars, reflecting its consistent performance and risk-adjusted returns. However, past performance is not indicative of future results, and investors should consult with a registered investment adviser before making any investment decisions. Additionally, Morningstar provides environmental, social, and governance (ESG) data regarding the securities held in the trust, offering valuable insights for socially conscious investors.

QQQ's performance is subject to ordinary brokerage commissions, and its current performance may vary, potentially higher or lower than previous periods. While QQQ is one of the most actively traded ETFs, investors should note that investing involves risks, and there is no guarantee of any future performance. Nevertheless, with its focus on large-cap growth stocks and its alignment with the NASDAQ-100 Index®, QQQ remains a prominent choice for investors seeking exposure to the technology and growth sectors.
