IWM Overview - The iShares Russell 2000 ETF

The iShares Russell 2000 ETF (IWM) is an equity exchange-traded fund that aims to track the performance of the Russell 2000 Index, which represents small-cap stocks in the United States. As an ETF, IWM offers investors exposure to a diversified portfolio of small-cap companies, providing potential for growth and diversification benefits.

IWM's investment strategy focuses on high dividend yield stocks within the small-cap segment, making it attractive to investors seeking income alongside capital appreciation. The fund's payment frequency is quarterly, and it has a track record of consistent dividend payments, making it appealing to income-focused investors.

With a geographic focus on the US domestic market, IWM reflects the performance of small-cap companies primarily operating within the United States. Its expense ratio of 0.33% is relatively low compared to many other ETFs, enhancing its appeal to cost-conscious investors.

iShares by BlackRock sponsors IWM, providing investors with the backing of a reputable financial institution known for its expertise in ETF management and investment solutions.

The fund's geographic focus on the US domestic market and a relatively low expense ratio of 0.33% make it an attractive choice for investors seeking diversified small-cap exposure with growth potential.
