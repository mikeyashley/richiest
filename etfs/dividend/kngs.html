KNGS Overview - The Roundhill S&P Dividend Monarchs ETF

The Roundhill S&P Dividend Monarchs ETF (KNGS) is an Equity Dividend ETF that focuses on providing investors with exposure to high dividend-yielding international stocks. Its investment strategy revolves around selecting stocks with a history of consistent dividend payments, aiming to capture dividend income while seeking potential capital appreciation. KNGS adopts a quarterly payment frequency for dividends, offering investors a regular income stream.

Geographically, KNGS has a global focus, allowing investors to diversify across various international markets. With an expense ratio of 0.25%, KNGS aims to provide cost-effective exposure to international dividend-paying equities.

Amplify ETFs serves as the sponsor for KNGS, managing the fund's operations and investment strategies. As an Equity Dividend ETF, KNGS targets companies with strong fundamentals and a commitment to dividend growth, potentially making it an attractive option for income-focused investors seeking international diversification in their portfolios.

Overall, KNGS offers investors the opportunity to gain exposure to international dividend-paying stocks with the potential for enhanced income and portfolio diversification.
