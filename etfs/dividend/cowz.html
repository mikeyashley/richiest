COWZ Overview - The Pacer US Cash Cows 100 ETF

The Pacer US Cash Cows 100 ETF (COWZ) is designed to capture the performance of U.S. companies with consistent cash flow, strong balance sheets, and attractive dividend yields. This ETF aims to provide investors with income from dividend payments while also offering potential for growth.

COWZ invests in companies that demonstrate financial stability and have a history of distributing dividends. The ETF focuses on established companies domiciled in the United States, with market capitalizations exceeding a certain threshold. By investing in a diversified portfolio of dividend-paying stocks, COWZ seeks to generate a reliable stream of income for investors.

Managed by Pacer Financial, COWZ employs a systematic approach to identify companies with robust cash flows and dividend growth potential. The fund's investment strategy aims to deliver consistent returns over the long term, aligning with investors' goals of income generation and capital appreciation.

As of Apr 30, 2024, COWZ had a dividend yield of 1.87%, providing investors with the opportunity to earn income on their investment. Investors can track COWZ's performance, including its dividend history and stock price movements, through financial platforms such as Morningstar and TipRanks.

While past performance is not indicative of future results, COWZ's focus on cash-rich companies with attractive dividend yields may appeal to investors seeking income generation and potential growth opportunities in the U.S. equity market.
