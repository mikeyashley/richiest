PBP Overview - The Invesco S&P 500 BuyWrite ETF

The Invesco S&P 500 BuyWrite ETF (PBP) is an exchange-traded fund designed to generate income while potentially providing capital growth. Managed actively, PBP aims to exceed the average yield on U.S. stocks while maintaining the potential for long-term appreciation. It primarily invests in equity securities of companies with market capitalizations exceeding $4.0 billion, focusing on dividend-paying stocks of established U.S. companies.

PBP employs a fundamental approach to identify attractively valued companies with the potential for consistent dividend growth. The fund’s portfolio is segmented and managed by individual managers, enabling diversification and strategic investment decisions.

As of May 9, 2024, PBP had an average yield of 5.2%. Investors can track PBP's performance, including its stock price and dividend history, through various platforms such as Morningstar and TipRanks.

While past performance does not guarantee future results, PBP's investment strategy may appeal to investors seeking income that surpasses the average yield of the broader market, along with the potential for sustained principal growth.
