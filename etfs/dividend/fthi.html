FTHI Overview - The First Trust BuyWrite Income ETF

The First Trust BuyWrite Income ETF (FTHI) is a meticulously managed investment vehicle aimed at generating income surpassing the average yield on U.S. stocks while maintaining potential for principal growth. This ETF allocates at least 90% of its assets into equity securities of companies with market capitalizations exceeding $4.0 billion, primarily focusing on dividend-paying stocks of established U.S. domiciled corporations.

Employing a fundamental approach, FTHI's investment adviser meticulously selects attractively valued companies capable of consistent dividend growth. The portfolio is structured into segments, each managed by individual managers empowered to make independent investment decisions, thereby ensuring diversification.

As of April 2, 2024, FTHI boasted an average yield of 1.54%, as reported by Morningstar. Investors keenly track its latest stock price and dividend history via platforms like TipRanks.

While past performance doesn't guarantee future results, FTHI's strategy of investing in high-quality, dividend-paying stocks of larger companies holds appeal for investors seeking income surpassing the market average yield, coupled with potential long-term principal growth.
