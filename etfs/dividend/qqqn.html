QQQN Overview - The VictoryShares Nasdaq Next 50 ETF

The VictoryShares Nasdaq Next 50 ETF (QQQN), also known as the QQQN fund, belongs to the family of exchange-traded funds (ETFs). It focuses on mid-cap growth stocks listed on the Nasdaq stock exchange. QQQN aims to provide investors with exposure to innovative companies poised for future success.

QQQN's investment strategy incorporates a blend of fundamental analysis and technical indicators to identify promising opportunities. Investors can analyze QQQN's performance and trading data using platforms like Morningstar and Yahoo Finance.
