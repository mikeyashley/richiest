The Bottom Line Up Front

Quick take: UWM is a 2x leveraged small-cap ETF for short-term tactical traders — not a long-term hold, but a powerful tool for magnified small-cap bets.

UWM (ProShares Ultra Russell2000) delivers twice (2x) the daily return of the Russell 2000 Index. That means double the gains on up days — and double the losses on down days. It's designed for short-term tactical trades by experienced investors, not as a buy-and-hold position. Compounding effects make UWM increasingly unpredictable over longer holding periods.

UWM Explained: What It Is and Why It Matters

Want to turbocharge your small-cap bet — with the full understanding that losses are also doubled? That's UWM.

UWM uses financial derivatives (swaps, futures) to achieve 2x the daily performance of the Russell 2000 Index (2,000 U.S. small-cap stocks). It rebalances daily, which means over time, the effects of compounding can cause UWM to diverge significantly from 2x the Russell 2000's total return. Managed by ProShares with a 0.95% expense ratio, UWM is a trading instrument — not a traditional income vehicle.

You're using UWM to offset short exposure elsewhere in your portfolio, or to rebalance sector tilts quickly. Leveraged ETFs can serve as efficient tactical hedging tools for professional-grade portfolio management.

The Seasoned Risk Taker

You understand volatility decay, daily rebalancing math, and leveraged ETF mechanics. You allocate a small portion of risk capital to UWM knowing that the position could go to near-zero if small-caps tank hard.

UWM Overview - The ProShares Ultra Russell2000

ProShares Ultra Russell2000 (UWM) is designed to provide daily investment results, before fees and expenses, that correspond to twice (200%) the daily performance of the Russell 2000® Index. The fund seeks to achieve this leveraged exposure through the use of financial instruments, including derivatives, that provide two times the return of the underlying index. This makes UWM a high-risk, high-reward investment option suitable for traders looking to capitalize on short-term movements in small-cap stocks.

As part of the ProShares family, UWM offers investors an opportunity to gain enhanced exposure to the small-cap sector, which includes companies with a lower market capitalization relative to the broader market. The Russell 2000® Index comprises 2000 of the smallest companies in the Russell 3000® Index, representing a wide array of industries and sectors.

UWM is particularly attractive for those who anticipate upward trends in the small-cap market and are willing to accept the increased volatility and potential for significant losses. The fund's performance is aimed at providing magnified returns over short periods, making it less suitable for long-term holding due to the effects of compounding and market fluctuations. Investors should carefully consider their investment strategy and risk tolerance before investing in ProShares Ultra Russell2000 (UWM).

It is essential to monitor ex-dividend dates and understand the implications of dividend policies on your investment strategy. Although UWMC offers a compelling dividend yield range, investors should remain vigilant about the company's earnings per share and overall growth rate to ensure the continued ability to pay a dividend.
