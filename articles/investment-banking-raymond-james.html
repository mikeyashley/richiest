What is the Role of an Investment Banking Analyst at Raymond James?

Day-to-Day Responsibilities of an Investment Banking Analyst

An investment banking analyst at Raymond James plays a crucial role in supporting senior bankers and clients. Their daily tasks include:

  • Financial Modeling: Building and maintaining financial models to project future financial performance.
  • Preparing Pitch Books: Creating presentation materials for client meetings and pitches.
  • Conducting Market Research: Analyzing industry trends and market data to support client recommendations.
  • Analyzing Financial Statements: Reviewing and interpreting financial statements to assess the financial health of companies.
  • Providing Detailed Reports and Presentations: Preparing comprehensive reports and presentations for senior team members and clients.
  • Supporting Mergers and Acquisitions: Assisting in the execution of mergers and acquisitions, including due diligence and valuation.
  • Capital Raising: Helping clients raise capital through equity and debt financing.
