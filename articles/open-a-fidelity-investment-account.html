How Do I Open a Fidelity Brokerage Account?

What Are the Steps to Open an Account?

Opening a Fidelity brokerage account is straightforward. Follow these steps to get started:

  1. Visit the Fidelity Website: Go to the Fidelity Investments homepage.
  2. Select "Open an Account": Click on the "Open an Account" button located on the main page.
  3. Choose Account Type: Select the type of account you wish to open (e.g., individual, joint, retirement).
  4. Complete the Application: Fill out the online application form with your personal information.
  5. Review and Submit: Review your information for accuracy and submit your application.
  6. Fund Your Account: Add funds to your new account to start investing.

What Documents Are Needed to Open a Fidelity Account?

To open a Fidelity brokerage account, you will need the following documents:

  • Personal Identification: A valid government-issued ID (e.g., driver's license, passport).
  • Social Security Number: Your SSN for tax reporting purposes.
  • Bank Information: Your bank account and routing numbers for funding your brokerage account.
  • Employment Information: Details about your employment status and income.

How Long Does It Take to Open a Brokerage Account?

The process of opening a Fidelity brokerage account typically takes about 10-15 minutes to complete the online application. After submission, the account is usually approved within 1-2 business days. Once approved, you can fund your account and begin investing.
