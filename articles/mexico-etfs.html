Investment Trusts vs. ETFs in the Mexican Market

Investment trusts and ETFs present different mechanisms for investing in the Mexican market. Investment trusts, closed-ended funds that trade on stock exchanges, can offer a fixed investment over a long period, potentially trading at a discount or premium to NAV. This structure contrasts with ETFs that provide liquidity and flexibility, trading throughout the day at market prices. Understanding these differences is crucial for investors determining the best vehicle for their investment strategy in Mexico.
