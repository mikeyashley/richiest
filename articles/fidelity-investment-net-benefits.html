How Do I Register for a Fidelity NetBenefits® Account?

Registering for a Fidelity NetBenefits® account is straightforward and ensures you can manage your workplace benefits and retirement plans efficiently. Follow these steps to get started. By following the steps below, you can quickly set up your account and start taking advantage of the benefits it offers.

Step-by-Step Guide to Registering

  1. Visit the Registration Page: Go to the Fidelity NetBenefits® registration page.
  2. Enter Your Information: Provide your personal information, including your Social Security number, date of birth, and email address.
  3. Create a Username and Password: Choose a username and password that you'll use to access your account.
  4. Set Up Security Questions: Select and answer security questions to help protect your account.
  5. Review and Submit: Check your information for accuracy and submit your registration.

Once you complete these steps, you'll receive a confirmation email with further instructions.

Common Issues and How to Fix Them

  • Incorrect Information: Ensure all personal details are entered correctly. Double-check your Social Security number and date of birth.
  • Username Already Taken: If your chosen username is already in use, try adding numbers or variations.
  • Password Issues: Follow the password guidelines to create a strong password. It should include a mix of letters, numbers, and special characters.

Verifying Your Account: What You Need to Know

After registering, you'll need to verify your account. This step is crucial to ensure the security of your information.

  • Email Verification: Check your email for a verification message from Fidelity. Click the link provided to verify your email address.
  • Phone Verification: You may also need to verify your phone number. Follow the instructions sent via SMS or call.

By completing these verification steps, you'll secure full access to your Fidelity NetBenefits® account.
