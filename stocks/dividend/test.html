Overview

 VICI Properties Inc. (VICI), traded on the NYSE (NYSE: VICI), is a leading REIT specializing in experiential properties like casinos, resorts, and entertainment venues. They offer investors exposure to this dynamic sector through a diverse portfolio.

Business Description

 VICI's core business revolves around acquiring, owning, and managing high-quality experiential properties in the US and Canada. Their impressive portfolio boasts 92 properties, including:
  • 54 Gaming Properties: Iconic casinos and resorts like Caesars Palace Las Vegas, MGM Grand, and The Venetian Las Vegas.
  • 38 Non-Gaming Experiential Properties: Golf courses, concert venues, and even retail outlets offer a well-rounded portfolio.
VICI primarily generates income through rental agreements with operating partners, ensuring steady income and minimizing operational expenses. This allows them to focus on their core expertise - property acquisition and management.

History and Background

 Formed in 2017 through a spin-off from Caesars Entertainment Corporation, VICI has experienced remarkable portfolio expansion through strategic acquisitions and development projects. Key milestones include:
  • 2018: Expanded their reach in the East Coast gaming market by acquiring Harrah's Atlantic City and Bally's Atlantic City.
  • 2019: Cemented their position as a major player in the Las Vegas Strip scene by acquiring MGM Grand Las Vegas and The Venetian Las Vegas.
  • 2020: Further solidified their position as a leading experiential REIT by acquiring 19 properties from Caesars Entertainment Corporation.

Market Presence

 VICI holds a strong market presence within the experiential real estate sector, particularly in the United States. They hold a significant share of the U.S. gaming market, particularly on the Las Vegas Strip, where they own some of the most iconic and recognizable properties. Additionally, VICI's expansion into non-gaming properties offers investors exposure to a broader range of leisure and entertainment trends, diversifying their portfolio and mitigating risk.

Competitive Advantages

 VICI enjoys several competitive advantages that ensure their continued success:
  • High-quality portfolio of flagship properties: Owning iconic destinations that attract significant tourism ensures consistent cash flow and long-term value.
  • Long-term leases with leading operators: Strong relationships with established operators mitigate operational risks and guarantee stable income streams.
  • Diversification across geographic markets and property types: VICI's geographically diversified portfolio includes a mix of gaming and non-gaming properties, providing resilience to market fluctuations and economic downturns.
