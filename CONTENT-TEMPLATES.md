# Richiest Content Templates (v1)

Use one of these two templates for all daily updates.

## 1) ETF Highlight Template

**Target page type:** `/etfs/.../*.html`

### Required structure
1. **SEO title** (clear query + year)
2. **Meta description** (benefit + intent)
3. **H1** (plain-English ETF focus)
4. **BLUF / Bottom line** (who it’s for, who it’s not for)
5. **Key facts table**
   - Ticker
   - Asset class
   - Expense ratio
   - Distribution frequency
   - Use case
6. **Pros / cons**
7. **Who should buy / avoid**
8. **Alternatives section** (2–3 related ETFs)
9. **FAQ** (3–5 intent questions)
10. **Related internal links** (3 links)

### SEO rules
- Include primary keyword in `<title>`, `<h1>`, first paragraph.
- Keep title under ~60 chars when possible.
- Keep meta description ~140–160 chars.

---

## 2) Regular Article Template

**Target page type:** `/articles/*.html`

### Required structure
1. **SEO title** (problem + comparison/outcome)
2. **Meta description** (what user gets)
3. **H1** (direct answer framing)
4. **Quick answer box** (3 bullets)
5. **Main body** (clear sections, no fluff)
6. **Decision framework / checklist**
7. **Common mistakes**
8. **FAQ**
9. **Related internal links** (3 links)

### SEO rules
- Match search intent exactly (comparison, definition, best-of, how-to).
- Add one internal link near top + two in body.
- Avoid generic intros longer than 100 words.
